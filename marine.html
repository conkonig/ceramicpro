The Challenge

The unrelenting marine environment

Salt water, UV and biological fouling are the greatest enemies of any vessel. Without protection, gelcoat becomes porous, chalky and susceptible to permanent damage. Ceramic Pro Marine is the world's first all-inclusive protective and preventative maintenance product, specially designed for professionals.

Waterline Staining

Marine scum, dock rash and exhaust emissions damage the surface and look unsightly.

visibility

Glass & Acrylic Windscreens

Salt staining and hazy swirl marks reduce clarity — a hazard during sunstrike and night vision.

Wax & polymer sealants fall short

Wax offers protection that lasts 3–6 months and can eventually react with the gelcoat causing chalking and fading.

Polymer sealants last 8 months to a year, help with UV damage, but will still break down and lose shine.

Ceramic Pro Marine bonds permanently — once cured, it cannot be washed away or chemically removed.
