What is Ceramic Pro?

Ceramic Pro is a clear, liquid nanoceramic coating that can be applied in multiple layers. After complete polymerization, the solutions on the surface transform into a permanent, durable but flexible glass shield.

3× the hardness

Of standard clear coats. Ceramic Pro 9H lifts surface hardness above 9H, providing superior scratch resistance and a permanent, mirror-like depth.

Unlike traditional waxes or sealants that degrade within months, Ceramic Pro forms a covalent bond with the substrate, creating a permanent protective layer that cannot be washed off.
